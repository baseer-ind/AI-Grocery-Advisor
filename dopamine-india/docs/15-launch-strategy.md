# Launch Strategy

## Pre-launch

- Closed beta (500–1000 invite-only users) sourced from no-spend/savings
  communities and student groups — natural fit for the "avoid impulse
  spend" value prop.
- Legal/trademark clearance pass on all brand names before any public
  store listing (see `legal-and-branding-safety.md`).
- App Store / Play Store review notes pre-written explaining "no real
  payments" explicitly, to avoid review friction.

## Positioning

"Window-shop guilt-free." Lead messaging with the savings/dopamine angle,
not "we copy Swiggy/Amazon" — own the *original* niche: anti-impulse-
spending entertainment, not a commerce clone.

## Channels

- India-focused content: Instagram Reels/YouTube Shorts showing the
  "delivered → you saved ₹X" payoff moment (highly shareable, screen-
  recordable by design).
- Personal-finance creator partnerships (no-spend challenge influencers).
- Reddit/Discord communities around frugality, financial wellness.
- ASO: keywords around "no spend," "savings tracker," "shopping
  simulator," "stress relief," explicitly avoiding any real brand names.

## Phased rollout

1. Closed beta → Phase 1 metrics gate (see `12-mvp-scope.md` exit
   criteria).
2. Open beta in 2–3 metro cities (Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi-NCR) — staged
   server capacity ramp.
3. Pan-India GA with Hindi localization live.
4. Paid acquisition only after organic D7 retention benchmark is hit
   (avoid buying users into a leaky funnel).

## Monetization (explicitly non-transactional)

No real payments ever. Future monetization candidates (V2+, evaluated
separately): non-intrusive sponsored "fictional brand" placements,
premium cosmetic themes/avatars, or a freemium tier for advanced AI
moodboard generation — all reviewed against store policy and the "no real
commerce" brand promise before implementation.
