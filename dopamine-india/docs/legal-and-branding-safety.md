# Legal & Branding Safety Rules

These rules are binding for every contributor (human or AI) working on
Dopamine India. When in doubt, choose the more original, more distinct
option.

## Hard prohibitions

- No real company names, trademarks, logos, mascots, slogans, fonts,
  splash screens, icons, sound effects, or product photography from any
  real brand (food delivery, e-commerce, travel, OTT, ride-hailing,
  ticketing, services, etc.).
- No pixel-for-pixel layout cloning of any specific app.
- No scraping or display of real retailer product catalogs/images without
  explicit licensing.
- No real brand color palette replication (e.g. a specific orange+white
  combo associated with one delivery app is off-limits even if "inspired
  by it").

## Required per fictional brand

1. Original name (see `brands.md` for the seed list of 200+).
2. Original wordmark/logo (generated, not derived from a real logo).
3. Original primary/secondary color pair, generated per-brand, validated
   against WCAG AA contrast.
4. Original iconography style (consistent icon grid/stroke width across
   the ecosystem, but no 1:1 icon copies from any real app's icon set).
5. Optional original mascot — must not resemble any existing brand mascot.

## Interaction patterns vs. visual design

Generic commerce/booking interaction patterns (cart → checkout → tracking,
seat maps, swipeable cards, bottom tab nav) are usability conventions and
are safe to use. What is NOT safe is copying a *specific* app's exact
visual treatment of those patterns (exact spacing, iconography, animation
curves, illustration style, copywriting voice tied to a real brand).

## Mandatory disclaimer

Shown once during onboarding (dismissible, logged as accepted) and always
available in Settings → Legal:

> "This application is an independent entertainment and financial wellness
> experience. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or
> associated with any real company, retailer, delivery platform, travel
> provider, or service mentioned or implied. All brands, stores, products,
> and services shown in the app are fictional or independently created for
> simulation purposes."

## Store compliance checklist (pre-launch gate)

- [ ] No trademarked terms in app name, screenshots, or store listing copy.
- [ ] No real brand logos anywhere in store assets.
- [ ] Privacy policy + data-deletion flow present (Play/App Store
      requirement).
- [ ] In-app purchases: none exist (no real payment processing at all —
      review notes should state this explicitly to reviewers).
- [ ] Age rating reflects "simulated commerce, no gambling, no real
      transactions."
- [ ] Legal review of `brands.md` names against existing live trademarks
      in relevant Nice classes before public launch (automated TM search +
      manual counsel pass).
