# V3 Features

Expansion + retention-deepening once social/gamification loops are
working.

- Remaining categories: Beauty, Electronics, Cars, Real Estate, Services,
  Jewellery, Books, Music, Kids, Pets, Gifts, Healthcare.
- Configurators: car builder with EMI calculator, electronics dream-setup
  builder, real-estate interior package builder.
- Cross-category AI planner ("plan my dream wedding/home office under
  ₹X" spanning multiple categories into one moodboard + multi-cart flow).
- Additional regional languages beyond English/Hindi (Tamil, Telugu,
  Bengali, Marathi — prioritized by signup geography).
- Richer social: group challenges, comment threads on shared dream carts,
  collaborative moodboards (multi-editor).
- Personalization engine: recommendation ranking based on saved/abandoned
  patterns (still simulation-only — no real targeting of ads/offers
  outside the app).
- Export/share enhancements: animated savings recap (shareable video card,
  "year in review" style).
- Web companion (read-only profile/wishlist viewer for sharing outside the
  app).
